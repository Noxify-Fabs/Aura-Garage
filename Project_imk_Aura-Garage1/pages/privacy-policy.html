Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 27, 2023

At Aura Garage, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Create an account on our website
  • Request information about our vehicles or services
  • Schedule a test drive
  • Apply for financing
  • Contact us via email, phone, or contact forms
  • Subscribe to our newsletter

2. How We Use Your Information

We may use the information we collect for various purposes, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our services
  • Process and complete transactions
  • Send you promotional materials and other communications
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and requests
  • Improve our website and services
  • Monitor and analyze usage and trends
  • Prevent fraudulent activities and enhance security

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who perform services on our behalf
  • Financial institutions for payment processing
  • Law enforcement or other government officials, in response to a verified request
  • Other parties in connection with a business transaction, such as a merger or acquisition

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access, update, or delete your information
  • The right to object to or restrict certain processing
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to withdraw consent

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our website and hold certain information. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
